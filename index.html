About Me

“My mission is to bridge the gap between policy and practice, ensuring that long-term care systems are not just efficient, but equitable and profoundly human.”

Dr. Darren Liu is a tenured Professor at West Virginia University's School of Public Health, where he leads research and education at the intersection of long-term care, health equity, and public health systems. With over two decades of academic experience spanning four universities, Dr. Liu brings a global perspective—shaped by his roots in Taiwan and his research across the United States, East Asia, and beyond—to every classroom, publication, and policy conversation. His work is driven by a commitment to person-centered care, reducing health disparities, and preparing the next generation of public health leaders through innovative, competency-based education.
